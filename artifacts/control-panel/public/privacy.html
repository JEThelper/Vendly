Privacy Policy — Vendly

Last updated: July 2026

Vendly ("we", "our") provides a WhatsApp-based ordering and messaging service that lets businesses ("Vendors") take orders and communicate with their customers via WhatsApp. This policy explains what data we collect, how it's used, and how it's protected.

What We Collect

  • Your WhatsApp phone number and display name, when you message a Vendor's number connected to Vendly.
  • The content of messages you send to place orders, ask questions, or otherwise communicate with a Vendor through our service.
  • Order details (items, quantities, totals) and basic order history with that Vendor.

How We Use It

  • To process and fulfill orders you place with a Vendor.
  • To respond to your messages, including using automated (AI-assisted) systems to understand order requests.
  • To help Vendors manage their own customers and order history.

Third-Party Processing

We use Meta's WhatsApp Business Platform to send and receive messages, and may use Google's Gemini API to help interpret order text. Messages processed by these services are subject to their respective privacy policies. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data Retention

Order and conversation records are retained to provide ongoing service to you and the Vendor. Temporary order-in-progress data is automatically deleted after a short window if an order isn't completed. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below.

Data Sharing Between Vendors

Each Vendor only has access to their own customers' data. Your information is not shared between different Vendors using Vendly.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
